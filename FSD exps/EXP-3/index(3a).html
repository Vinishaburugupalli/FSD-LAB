Career & Biography

Kim Tae-hyung, known professionally as V, was born on December 30, 1995 in Daegu, South Korea. He debuted as a member of the global K-pop group BTS in 2013 and is known for his deep, warm vocal tone, charismatic stage presence, and artistic creativity.

Music & Style

V's voice is often described as rich and soulful; he has contributed vocals and songwriting to many BTS tracks and to his solo releases. He blends genres — pop, jazz, R&B — and is celebrated for his experimental approach to melody and mood.

Acting

V made his acting debut with a supporting role in the historical drama Hwarang (2016). He continues to explore acting alongside his music career.

Notable Works & Solo Releases

  • Solo songs released with BTS and on soundtracks.
  • Solo tracks and collaborations that highlight his unique vocal color.
  • Frequent participation in songwriting and production.
